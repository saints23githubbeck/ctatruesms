New Template Tag

Ultimate SMS provideds certain template variables or tags that you can use to personalize message content. The most commonly used variables are personalization variables, which allow you to dynamically include information about your recipients. For example, their name, email address, or customer properties you've stored about the person. Like, Hi {{first_name}} {{lastName}}, you email is {{email}} to Hi John Doe, your email is john.does@gmail.com. Your customers will see these values when they will store contacts on their contact groups. Here, Name filed is for tag name like frist_name. Second, Type field is for attributed specifies the type of input element to display. Finally, Required field is for the Tag field is mandatory or not.
